About me #

I am a researcher in Cognitive Neuroscience, currently working for Radboudumc at the Donders Institute in Nijmegen, The Netherlands.

My main research interests include (real-time) fMRI, neurofeedback, large-scale brain networks, stress, resilience, embodied cognition and numerical cognition.

I am also the main developer of Expyriment, a Python library for designing and conducting timing-critical bahavioural and neuroimaging experiments.
